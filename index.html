What is APTURA?

APTURA is an immersive VR platform that helps customers visualize their dream home before it's built. We provide a seamless virtual tour experience for both buyers and builders.

Features That Redefine Virtual Touring

Immersive VR Apartment Tours

Step inside your future home with lifelike 3D virtual reality tours powered by Oculus Quest 2.

  • Smooth, intuitive navigation
  • Explore interiors like you're really there

Real-Time VR Casting to Mobile

Share the experience live with your family or friends—no headset required.

  • Stream your VR view directly to any Android phone
  • View-only mode for remote users

Explore Multiple Apartment Models

From cozy studios to luxury flats—explore all layouts in one app.

  • Visual selection of apartment types
  • 3D previews and smooth transitions

Ultra-Responsive & Seamless Experience

No lags. No glitches. Just smooth, high-quality virtual tours.

  • Works smoothly on Quest 2 and Android

Designed for Remote Decision-Making

  • Real-time viewing removes location barriers

Built with Smart Tech

  • Future-ready architecture
